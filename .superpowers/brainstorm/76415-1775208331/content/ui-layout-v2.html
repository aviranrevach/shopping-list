Key changes

  • Lists screen: "New list" is a subtle dashed row at the bottom — secondary action, not a FAB
  • List Detail: FAB for adding items — this is the high-frequency action while shopping
  • Items grouped by category with section headers
  • Checked items show with strikethrough and sink to bottom of their category
  • Tap a list card to drill in, back arrow to return
