How it works

  • Sticky search bar at the top — always visible, always ready
  • Type to search — filters from past items (across all family lists) with their remembered category
  • Tap a suggestion → adds it to the list with its last-used category & unit
  • "Add new" row at bottom of suggestions → creates a brand new item (prompts for category)
  • No FAB, no modal — the search bar IS the add flow
